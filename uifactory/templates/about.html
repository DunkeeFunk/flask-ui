Mission Statement

The declining value of the agriculture market in the developing world is becoming a concern on a global scale. Recent research into precision agriculture and pre-script farming has been on the agenda for many institutions hoping to avoid the rise in value of necessary commodities. Precision agriculture and pre-script farming are the process of analyzing data collected from the crops environment to make valuable insights and in turn aid decision making. This report aims to shed light on these issues and provide an essential solution. Many IoT systems have been created by international companies to aid the struggle of small and industrial sized farms fighting against extreme weather conditions and unfertile soil. The proposed solution will help farmers gain valuable insights into the physical environment of their plants by sensing temperature, light, soil moisture and humidity and allow farmers to make good business decisions. One such example would be to monitor the soil moisture and humidity to determine when plants should be watered.
